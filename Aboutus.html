About Our Shop

Welcome to Petals & Layers, your destination for stunning floral arrangements and irresistible cakes! Founded with a passion for creativity and celebration, our shop is a blend of artistry and flavor, designed to make every moment special.

Our Flowers

At Petals & Layers, we believe flowers have the power to express emotions words often cannot. From timeless roses to exotic orchids, we carefully select each bloom to ensure freshness, quality, and beauty. Whether you need a romantic bouquet, elegant event decor, or a simple arrangement to brighten your day, we craft each design with care and creativity.

Our Cakes

Every celebration deserves a sweet touch, and our cakes are baked to perfection to delight your taste buds. From rich chocolate layers to delicate fruit flavors, each cake is a masterpiece, both in taste and appearance. We specialize in customized designs, making your desserts as unique as your occasion.

Our Philosophy

We are more than just a shop—we’re a part of your celebrations, milestones, and memories. Our mission is to provide exceptional service, combining the freshest flowers and the finest cakes to create unforgettable experiences.
